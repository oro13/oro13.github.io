Kick Starting a Market Place Business

The second article of notes on Lenny Rachitskey's (ex-AirBnB) interview on the [Two-Sided podcast](https://www.sharetribe.com/twosided/)

Context: Lenny Rachitskey was an early employee of AirBnB and credited for playing a key role in growing operations to the scale we see today. When he left the company, many people started seeking him out for advice on their own company. Only being able to pull from his own experience at one company, he decided to reach out to key players at 17 (at the time of this interview) top market place tech companies that are well known today. These include Uber, Lyft, Thumbtack, Task Rabbit, Etsy, Patreon, and so on. He offers this advice with the disclaimer that there are no static, fool proof methods for succesful market platforms.
